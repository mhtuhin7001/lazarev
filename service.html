AI UX Design for VT News

A news portal that provides users with access to diverse perspectives on events and supports critical thinking and objective evaluation of information. Using artificial intelligence, it helps users quickly analyze and explore topics in depth.
Key insights into AI+News:

Created a news portal that provides access to diverse perspectives, helping users quickly analyze and explore topics using artificial intelligence.

Deep research to understand user behavior and preferences in news consumption, enabled us to develop a strategy for integrating advanced artificial intelligence algorithms aimed at helping users quickly access specific information, prioritizing content based on user interests and current topics.

AI UX Designer for Fintech. 2017-2024

Accern helps financial firms automate research and classify content for AI models. They needed a full UX and UI revamp, so we created their design from scratch, which delivered $40M over 7 years.
Key insights into AI+Fintech:

Users often struggle to organize data from financial research. That’s why we always add a “cluster” tool to gather information in a single place for easy access.

If you’re building a chat-based interface, make sure you use AI to predict and suggest prompts for easy UX.

Users often miss AI products' value due to hard-to-read documentation and irrelevant tutorials. Instead, use an AI chatbot that offers solutions based on user tasks in real time.
